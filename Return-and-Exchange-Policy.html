Return And Exchange Policy

Thank you for choosing TBJ Hair Creations. We appreciate your choice. Please take a moment to review our Return & Exchange Policy to ensure a smooth experience should you need to return a product. Your satisfaction is our priority.

General Conditions

  • Returns or exchanges are only accepted within 5 days of receiving the product.
  • Sale or discounted orders are not eligible for return or exchange.
  • A valid reason must be provided for the return or exchange.
  • After 5 days, no returns or exchanges will be processed.

Products Not Eligible for Return or Exchange

  • Sale Orders / Discounted Products
  • Products that have been colored, chemically treated, or damaged due to styling post-purchase

Return Process

  1. Initiate Return: Log in to your account on tbjhaircreations.com. Provide a brief explanation for the return and attach a picture if necessary.
  2. Approval: Our dedicated team will carefully review your request. If your return is deemed eligible, we will approve it and arrange for a reverse pickup.
  3. Quality Check (QC): Upon receiving the product, it will undergo a thorough quality check. If it meets our return criteria then we will arrange the replacement.

Important Instructions

  • Please ensure the product is in its original condition, with no signs of use or alteration. Products that have been washed, cut, colored, chemically treated, or brushed for curly or wavy hair will not be accepted.
  • To expedite the process for damaged or defective products, kindly record an unboxing video before opening the courier seal.
  • When returning the product, please clearly mention your order number and contact details on the package.
  • A deduction of INR 300 will be applied to cover logistics, prepping, and packaging costs for all returns/exchanges.

Contact Us For any inquiries or assistance, please do not hesitate to reach out to us at tbjhaircreations@gmail.com. Our team is here to ensure your satisfaction with every aspect of your TBJ Hair Creations experience.
