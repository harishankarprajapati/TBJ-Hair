Why TBJ Hair Creations

Quality from start to finish!

Best quality hair extensions can be made only with unprocessed Indian hair combined with professionally skilled workers and proper infrastructure. We just don't sell by labeling the hair, we make the hair extensions from collected hair of South Indian temples. Full control of the production process allows us to create premium hair extensions and wigs.

Step 1:- Hair Source

Hair are collected from south Indian temples. Hair purchased are checked for length, strength, bundle density, and hair color (hair color should be natural) and final consideration is lice and knits and split end free hair

Step 2:- Hair Sorting

First step in manufacturing is hair sorting it is also the most important stage in manufacturing of hair products. Hair sorting is the process of color segregations and length sizing.

Step 3:-Triple Head Stitching

Hair is a master craft men who is experienced stitching stiches the hair. The triple head stitching has an inbuilt glue box. So the stitching weft is already glued. When the glue has become dry. A second code of glue is applied on the glue machine .Once again the glue has become dry. It goes for double stitching.

Step 4:-First Wash

The hair is washed in good shampoo so that all the dirt on the hair is removed. After washing and drying the hair in the oven. They go for heckling process.

Step 5:- Pre or First Heckling Process

Here skilled labor carefully hackled the hair to remove the tangle if any. Then it go to washing again (second wash). In second wash the hair is again washed the shampoo and then in conditioner water then the hair rinsed water but we leave little conditioner in the hair. Now again the hair go for heckling. The hair now are dry in oven and send for final heckling

Step 6:-Final Hackling

In time the hackling process the hair is segregations for color, textures and length. The similar hair are maid in to110 gm bundles. After making 110 gm bundles the top head measure even. This is done so that all the hair comes from in the machine. Now these bundles go for triple head stitching.

Step 7:- Unskilled Labor

Unskilled labor takes out any lice and knits from the bundles there by making the weft clean and clear of lice and knits.

Step 8:- Double Wafting Machine

Glue applied weft is carefully stitched into double weft. Thereby making it strong and shading free. Now this readymade weft go to unskilled labour to takeout any lice and knits from the bundles..

Step 9:- Final Inspection

Now the weft go for final inspection here skilled supervisor and skilled packing In charge checked each bundle for shading and any lice or knits. If they find any problem with the bundles it goes back for changing, repair or discard
